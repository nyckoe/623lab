## Description

This review build is a functional local demo for care professionals. It uses fictional data and does not connect to production authentication, credential, payer, booking, claim, support, or deletion services.

The executable demo lets a reviewer:

- advance learning and open original preparation content;
- keep course completion separate from licenses and credentials;
- complete a fictional payer-review step and see shared-policy activation blockers change;
- accept or decline fictional work only when activation policy allows;
- advance a visit checklist through documentation submission;
- open fictional earnings and claim detail;
- queue a local support request and toggle local sign-in state;
- initiate account deletion, confirm it explicitly, and see a submitted/pending request state.

CKare Pro does not grant a license. Insurance verification and claim acceptance do not guarantee payment. CKare is not emergency dispatch.

Authentication, persistent data, credential/payer sources, bookings, claims, support delivery, hosted legal pages, and server-side deletion are owner-controlled production integrations required before a production service launch.
