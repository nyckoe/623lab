Title: CKare Pro

Short description: Explore fictional care-work qualification, visit, claim, and request states.

## Full description

This build is a functional local demo with fictional professional, booking, credential, and claim data.

EXECUTABLE DEMO FEATURES

• Learning progress and original preparation content
• Shared-policy activation blockers and fictional credential progress
• Policy-gated work acceptance, decline, checklist, and documentation states
• Fictional earnings and claim detail
• Local support-request and sign-in-state controls
• Explicit account-deletion confirmation ending in submitted/pending

CKare Pro does not grant a license. Insurance verification and claim acceptance do not guarantee payment. CKare is not emergency dispatch.

Production authentication, persistent storage, credential/payer data, booking, claims, support delivery, privacy links, and server-side deletion remain owner-controlled integrations.
