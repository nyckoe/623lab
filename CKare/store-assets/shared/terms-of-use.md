# CKare Terms of Use — production draft

**Owner action required:** Complete legal-entity, governing-law, dispute, payment, refund, cancellation, age, and contact provisions with qualified counsel before publication.

## Service

CKare provides technology for qualification workflows, verified-status display, care matching, booking, documentation, coverage and claim administration, and related support. CKare's precise legal role depends on the approved operating model and contracts.

## No license or professional guarantee

Course completion is not a license, certificate, regulator approval, authorization to practice, payer enrollment, or marketplace activation. Users remain responsible for satisfying jurisdiction, employer, payer, and professional requirements.

## Coverage and payment

Insurance verification and benefit information are estimates and do not guarantee payment. Network, referral, order, authorization, medical necessity, frequency, place of service, coordination of benefits, claim processing, and other rules may affect coverage and final responsibility.

## Emergency boundary

CKare is not emergency dispatch and must not be used for an immediate medical, safety, abuse, neglect, or crisis emergency. Users should contact local emergency services or the appropriate crisis or protective resource.

## Accounts and authority

Users must provide accurate information, protect credentials, use only their own account, and act for another person only with valid authority. Provider users may offer only services within verified legal and contractual scope.

## Bookings and records

Users must review rates, estimates, cancellation terms, required tasks, authorization status, consent, and known needs before confirmation. Required professional and operational records must be accurate and timely.

## Payments and subscriptions

Digital training subscriptions purchased in mobile apps follow Apple or Google billing terms. Real-world care services follow the disclosed payer, program, or private-payment path. The final terms must state pricing, fees, cancellation, refund, denial, appeal, and recoupment rules.

## Suspension and termination

CKare may restrict services when credentials expire, safety or fraud concerns arise, payer or regulator status changes, or these terms are violated. Users receive applicable notice and appeal/support routes.

## Privacy and deletion

Data use follows the published privacy policy. Users may request data export and account deletion; legally required retained records are explained during the deletion process.
