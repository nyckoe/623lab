# CKare Support, Data Export, and Account Deletion page copy

Publish this content at stable public URLs before submission, with working forms or authenticated workflows.

## Support

Choose the product you use: CKare Pro or CKare Care. Provide your account email, a short description, and whether the issue involves access, credentials, coverage, booking, safety, payment, or privacy. Do not send passwords, full payment-card information, or unnecessary care details.

CKare is not emergency dispatch. For an immediate medical or safety emergency, contact local emergency services. Insurance verification does not guarantee payment.

## Request a data export

Submit the account email and product. CKare verifies identity before preparing the data export. The response explains included records, secure delivery, and any records controlled by another legally responsible entity.

## Request account deletion

Users can initiate Delete account in each app under Profile → Privacy and support. A public request form must also allow a former user to request deletion without reinstalling the app.

The flow must:

1. identify CKare Pro or CKare Care and the account email;
2. verify identity through an existing trusted channel;
3. offer deletion of the account and associated eligible data, not only deactivation;
4. explain subscriptions that continue through Apple or Google and link to subscription management;
5. revoke Sign in with Apple tokens when applicable;
6. identify records retained for legal, payer, tax, safety, fraud-prevention, or audit obligations and state the reason and period;
7. confirm receipt and completion through the verified contact channel.

The production page must name the legal entity, expected response timeframe, privacy contact, and mailing address.
