# CKare Privacy Policy — production draft

**Owner action required:** Insert the legal entity name, effective date, postal address, privacy email, service locations, subprocessors, and approved retention schedule after legal review. Publish the completed policy at a stable public URL before any store submission.

## Scope

This policy covers CKare Pro, CKare Care, and their related web services. CKare Pro supports professional qualification and care-work operations. CKare Care supports coverage readiness, provider matching, bookings, and care coordination.

## Information CKare may collect

Depending on the product and feature used, CKare may collect account and contact information, identity-verification results, professional credentials and status, representative authority, minimum necessary care and accessibility information, insurance and authorization metadata, bookings, visit documentation, claims and payout metadata, communications, consents, privacy requests, device information, usage events, and diagnostics.

CKare should not collect information that is not necessary for the selected feature. Payment processors retain full card or bank credentials; CKare stores only required references and status metadata.

## How information is used

CKare uses information to authenticate accounts, verify status, enforce service scope, match eligible services, coordinate bookings, support documentation and claims, process payments, provide support, prevent fraud and abuse, maintain safety and compliance, improve reliability, and meet legal obligations.

## Sharing and processors

CKare may disclose minimum necessary information to providers, authorized representatives, payer and eligibility partners, payment processors, identity and credential vendors, hosting and communications providers, support vendors, regulators, and legal authorities when required. CKare does not sell personal information or use sensitive care information for targeted advertising.

## Retention

Account and operational data is retained only as long as needed for the stated purpose and applicable legal, payer, tax, fraud-prevention, audit, or recordkeeping obligations. The final published policy must list approved periods or criteria for each record class.

## Security

CKare uses encryption in transit, role-based access, row-level database controls, audit events, environment-separated credentials, and restricted administrative access. No security method eliminates every risk.

## User choices

Users can review profile information, manage notifications and representatives, request a data export, and initiate account deletion in the app. Account deletion removes the account and eligible associated data; CKare explains information that must be retained and why. If Sign in with Apple is used, deletion also revokes the associated token.

Data export and account deletion are also available through the public privacy-request page for people who no longer have the app.

## Product boundaries

CKare training completion is not a license or regulator approval. Insurance verification does not guarantee payment. CKare is not emergency dispatch or a substitute for emergency services.

## Contact

The published policy must provide the legal entity's privacy contact and mailing address.
