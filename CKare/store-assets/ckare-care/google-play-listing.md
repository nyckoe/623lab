# CKare Care — Google Play listing

Title: CKare Care

Short description: Explore fictional household care, coverage, booking, and request states.

## Full description

This build is a functional local demo with fictional household, coverage, provider, and visit data.

EXECUTABLE DEMO FEATURES

• Complete-record switching between two fictional household people
• Shared-policy coverage blockers and disabled protected actions
• Routine care routing and fictional booking requests
• Urgent and self-harm routing to 911/988 guidance outside matching
• Local visit checklist, coverage-help, support, and sign-in controls
• Explicit account-deletion confirmation ending in submitted/pending

Insurance verification does not guarantee payment. CKare is not emergency dispatch.

Production authentication, representative authority, persistent storage, payer/provider data, booking, support delivery, privacy links, and server-side deletion remain owner-controlled integrations.
