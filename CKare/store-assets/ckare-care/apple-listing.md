## Description

This review build is a functional local demo for people coordinating care. It uses fictional data and does not connect to production authentication, representative-authority, payer, provider, booking, support, or deletion services.

The executable demo lets a reviewer:

- switch between Maya and Leo and see the complete person record change across every tab;
- see shared-policy coverage blockers and disabled protected actions;
- route a routine request and submit a fictional booking request when allowed;
- route urgent or self-harm language away from matching to 911/988 guidance;
- advance a local visit checklist;
- queue local coverage-help and support requests;
- toggle local sign-in state;
- initiate account deletion, confirm it explicitly, and see a submitted/pending request state.

Insurance verification does not guarantee payment. CKare is not emergency dispatch.

Authentication, representative authority, persistent household data, payer/provider sources, bookings, support delivery, hosted legal pages, and server-side deletion are owner-controlled production integrations required before a production service launch.
