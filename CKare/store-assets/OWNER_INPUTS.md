# Owner-supplied inputs required before submission

Do not store secrets, passwords, signing keys, certificates, provisioning profiles, service-account JSON, or production tokens in this repository.

## Business and legal

- Legal entity name, address, support contact, privacy contact, and authorized signer.
- Approved provider-of-record operating model and any required agency/entity licenses.
- Jurisdiction, service, payer, worker-classification, fee, record-custody, and retention approvals.
- Final privacy policy, terms, consent language, complaint process, and account-deletion process reviewed by counsel.
- Tax, banking, payout, and claims arrangements.

## Apple

- Active Apple Developer membership and App Store Connect access.
- Confirmed bundle identifier ownership for `com.ckare.pro` and `com.ckare.care`.
- App Store Connect app IDs, team ID, signing certificates, and provisioning profiles managed through EAS/Apple.
- Pricing, territories, age-rating answers, encryption answers, app privacy answers, and content-rights attestations.
- Public privacy policy URL, support URL, marketing URL, and user privacy choices/account deletion URL.
- Functional reviewer account for each app and a monitored review contact.

## Google

- Verified Google Play Console developer account.
- Confirmed application IDs `com.ckare.pro` and `com.ckare.care`.
- Play App Signing enrollment and secure service-account access for automated submission if used.
- Pricing, territories, target audience, content rating, Health apps declaration, Data Safety answers, and policy attestations.
- Public privacy policy URL, support contact, website, and functional account-deletion URL.
- Functional reviewer account for each app and App access instructions.

## Platform services

- Production Supabase project URL and publishable client key; service role stays server-only.
- Applied migration, validated row-level policies, backups, monitoring, incident response, and approved retention jobs.
- Stripe and RevenueCat production projects if their respective payment features are enabled.
- Apple/Google subscription product IDs for digital training only.
- Payer/eligibility, claims, identity, credential, communications, analytics, crash-reporting, and support vendor contracts and production credentials.
- Final data-flow inventory matching both privacy worksheets.

## Release content

- Final pricing and cancellation/refund terms.
- Approved screenshots from production-like fictional accounts.
- Production reviewer accounts with no real person, patient, provider, plan, claim, or payment data.
- Confirmed app names, trademarks, support mailbox, and store descriptions.
