What we do

We turn lived experience into safe, realistic practice — so caregivers learn the hardest conversations before they happen for real.

🧬

Data-Grounded Simulation

Simulated patients are built from real, consented patient experience — not textbook stereotypes — so practice reflects how symptoms actually show up, session after session.

🛡️

Trauma-Informed by Design

Every scenario and rubric is built on trauma-informed principles — safety, trust, choice, collaboration, and cultural humility — so training prevents harm instead of just documenting it.

🌏

Bilingual, Culturally Grounded

Built first for Chinese-speaking caregiver communities in NYC, with real code-switching support — not translation bolted on after the fact.

📈

Practice → Competency

Caregivers rehearse, receive rubric-scored debriefs grounded in real outcomes, and build a portable record of competency over time.
